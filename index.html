Tweet a Snowman

Tweet to @tweetasnowman to make your own snowman!

Tweet

Make your own

Tweet directly to @tweetasnowman with your instructions in any order. Refresh this page after tweeting. It takes the elves roughly 15-20 seconds to make each snowman after tweeting.

Examples

  • @tweetasnowman gold hat scarf winking
  • @tweetasnowman santa shiver two buttons tie
  • @tweetasnowman earmuffs #4ae9b8
  • @tweetasnowman pink earmuffs little
  • @tweetasnowman olive four buttons

Color

  • Valid color name
  • Hexadecimal color code

Accessories

  • tophat or santa hat or cap
  • earmuffs
  • scarf or tie or bow tie
  • zero buttons
  • one button
  • two buttons
  • three buttons
  • four buttons
  • pinocchio
  • tooth
  • teeth
  • armless

Behavior

  • wink
  • shiver
  • happy
